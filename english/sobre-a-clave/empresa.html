Experience

IN PROFILE MAPPING AND DEVELOPMENT

With 30 years of market experience in behavioral and potential assessment processes, combined with leading market practices, Clave has established itself as one of the top human resources consultancies in Brazil.

We operate across the entire employee journey, leveraging technology to drive innovation and deliver results. We are practical, objective, transparent, and ethical—earning the trust of candidates, employees, and organizations of all sizes and industries across Brazil.

Methodology

FOR PROFESSIONAL AND PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT

With an exclusive methodology focused on maximizing both personal and professional potential, Clave delivers highly effective profile mapping processes. These encompass a range of solutions built on ethical and professional exchanges, enabling deeper self-awareness.

Results

DRIVEN BY CUSTOMIZED STRATEGIES AND SOLUTIONS

Through tailored solutions developed from a deep understanding of each client’s strategy and challenges, Clave delivers meaningful results. We continuously invest in new technologies, tools, and practices to provide accurate professional insights and develop attraction, selection, and profile mapping initiatives aligned with both individual aspirations and business needs. Our model is grounded in high-quality analytical data, enabling the identification of high-potential and high-performance profiles across different levels and career stages, for both candidates and employees.
