FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS FROM OUR

partners

WHAT DO WE EXPECT FROM OUR PARTNERS?

We expect our partners to understand our business vision and purpose so that we can move forward together in a unified direction: simplifying behavioral assessment for HR, while enhancing the quality of analysis processes. We believe our partners are close to these operations and are able to identify what, in many cases, those facing challenges may not clearly see.

WHAT CAN OUR PARTNERS EXPECT FROM US?

Our partners expect us to solve the challenges faced by their clients, especially in behavioral profile assessment. Making recommendations carries responsibility, and partners expect us to uphold that trust with care and commitment. You can be confident that this aligns with our purpose and with over 30 years of established expertise behind our brand.

CAN CLAVE BE INCLUDED IN MY PRODUCT PORTFOLIO?

Yes. Many partners operate this way, including our brand in their websites as official partners. In other cases, when closing service packages with clients, partners recommend Clave’s solutions so clients can experience and benefit from our offerings.

WHAT ARE THE MAIN CHALLENGES CLAVE’S SOLUTIONS ADDRESS?

We simplify behavioral analysis with quality and precision at a cost aligned with each company’s needs and budget. Our solutions address common challenges faced by HR teams, such as overly complex or unqualified tools that hinder routines and compromise the effectiveness of behavioral assessment processes aligned with each organization’s culture.

WHO IS CLAVE’S TARGET AUDIENCE?

Small, medium, and large companies that require support in behavioral analysis and talent management to strengthen their strategic, managerial, and operational processes.

CAN I HAVE A REFERRAL AGREEMENT WITH CLAVE EVEN IF I AM NOT YET AN APPROVED PARTNER?

Yes. Regardless of being an approved partner or not, if a referral to Clave results in a closed deal, we can establish a referral agreement and define participation terms based on successful contract execution with new clients.

CAN I REFER A FRIEND OR COMPANY TO BECOME A PARTNER?

Of course. You can ask them to fill out the “BECOME A PARTNER” form or contact their account manager to learn more about becoming a Clave partner.
