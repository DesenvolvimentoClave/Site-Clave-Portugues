Privacy and Data Protection Policy

Clave Consultoria LTDA. (referred to in this policy as "Clave Consultoria", "Clave", “we”, “us” or "our") is a service provider in the human resources sector, with over 30 years of experience in behavioral assessment and profile mapping processes. Our goal is to understand our clients in order to offer solutions that best align with their needs. Our model is based on high-quality analytical data, enabling the identification of high-potential and high-performance profiles across different levels and career stages, for both candidates and employees. Therefore, the processing of Personal Data (as defined below) is an inherent part of our business activities.

"Clave" recognizes the importance of protecting Personal Data processed in the course of its activities. For this reason, we have developed this document, referred to as the Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”), which establishes, in accordance with Law No. 13,709/2018 (the Brazilian General Data Protection Law – “LGPD”), the rules and conditions under which your Personal Data is processed when you use "Clave's" services, whether through digital means or offline.

This Privacy Policy applies to individuals (natural persons) who interact with "Clave's" services as users or clients (“you”). It explains how your Personal Data is collected, used, and disclosed by "Clave". It also provides information on how you can access and update your Personal Data and make certain decisions regarding its use.

Through this Privacy Policy, we aim to provide transparency about the Personal Data we process, the reasons for such processing, and the methods used. We are also committed to ensuring the protection and security of Personal Data belonging to our clients and website users, in compliance with the LGPD and other applicable laws.

We strive to provide you with the best possible experience, following industry best practices in security and compliance with applicable regulations. Therefore, "Clave" reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time, whenever deemed necessary. The latest version will always be available on our website and software platforms.

By continuing to browse our website and/or use our software, you declare that you are at least 18 (eighteen) years old and fully capable of understanding and agreeing to this Privacy Policy. If you do not meet these requirements or do not agree with the terms set forth herein, you must immediately discontinue the use of our website and software.

Please read this Privacy Policy and Data Protection Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your Personal Data and how it is processed.

This Privacy Policy covers our data collection activities both online and offline, including Personal Data collected through various channels such as websites, software, social media, surveys, and events. Please note that we may combine Personal Data collected from different sources (for example, online and offline interactions). As part of this process, we may merge Personal Data originally collected through different Clave products or services, as well as through partners of Clave Consultoria LTDA. You may refer to the section “Your rights regarding Personal Data” for more information on how to object to such processing.

In certain cases, if you choose not to provide Personal Data identified as necessary (we will clearly indicate when this is the case, for example, in registration forms), we may not be able to provide our services to you.

Finally, you acknowledge that it is prohibited to provide Personal Data belonging to third parties. This restriction is intended to safeguard the rights of others. Therefore, you are solely and fully responsible for any Personal Data you provide through our website and software.

HOW TO INTERPRET THIS PRIVACY POLICY

Capitalized terms shall have the meanings assigned to them in this Privacy Policy and/or in the LGPD (Brazilian General Data Protection Law). For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, the following definitions apply:

Personal Data or Data: Any information relating to a Client and/or Website User that enables their identification, even if such identification requires the combination with other information, or that may be associated with such data, and which is subject to Processing Activities.

Processing Activities: Any activities and/or operations performed with Personal Data, including but not limited to collection, production, receipt, classification, use, access, reproduction, processing, storage, archiving, deletion, evaluation, modification, communication, transfer, or extraction of Personal Data.

Website User: Any natural person who accesses, browses, and/or uses the features and services available on our Website or Software, whether in a public or restricted environment (such as a logged-in area).

Purpose and Necessity: The reason why a Personal Data Processing Activity is required to achieve a specific objective.

Website: The website available at https://www.clavegroup.com.br through which Clients and/or Website Users can access information about the company and/or use its services.

Legal Basis for Processing: The legal grounds that support and legitimize the Processing Activities.

National Data Protection Authority (ANPD): The public authority responsible for overseeing, implementing, and enforcing compliance with the LGPD.

ABOUT THE USE OF COOKIES, LOG FILES AND SIMILAR TECHNOLOGIES

Cookies are small text files that may be placed on your computer or mobile device by websites or online services you use. They are used to ensure the proper functioning of websites and online services, as well as to provide information to the owners of those websites or services.

Cookies are used to improve the use and functionality of Clave's websites and services and to better understand how visitors interact with our platforms, tools, and services. Cookies help adapt Clave's websites to your personal needs, facilitate usability, and allow us to collect feedback on user satisfaction.

Clave uses the following types of cookies on its websites and compatible services:

Session cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. When you restart your browser and return to the website that created the cookie, you will be treated as a new visitor.

Persistent cookies: Cookies that remain on your browser until you delete them manually or until they expire according to their defined duration. These cookies recognize your return to a Clave website or service.

Strictly necessary cookies: Cookies that are essential for the operation of a Clave website or service. They enable navigation and the use of essential features.

Cookies that collect information about you: These cookies are placed on Clave websites or compatible services and are used to collect information about your interactions with our platforms.

Advertising cookies (Clave): We place cookies in advertisements displayed on third-party websites and services. Through these cookies, we collect information about your interaction with ads. Clave may place third-party cookies on its own websites and services, and use data collected by third-party cookies to deliver advertisements that we believe are relevant to your interests, based on your browsing behavior.

Third-party cookies: These are cookies placed on Clave websites by partner companies, such as online advertising providers. They may use the data collected through these cookies to anonymously deliver targeted advertisements on other websites, based on your visits to Clave websites or compatible services.

ABOUT CLAVE WEBSITES AND SERVICES, TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES AND SIMILAR TO COOKIES

Clave websites and compatible services may also use other tracking technologies similar to cookies, which may collect information such as IP addresses, log files, and web beacons, among others. These data are also used to help us adapt our websites and services to your personal needs.

Further details are provided below:

IP Addresses: An IP address is a number used on the internet or a network to identify your computer. Whenever you connect to the internet, your device receives an IP address assigned by your internet service provider. We may record IP addresses for the following purposes:

  • Troubleshooting technical issues;
  • Maintaining the security and protection of our websites and online services;
  • Gaining a better understanding of how our websites and services are used;
  • Adapting content based on your geographic location.

Log Files: Clave, or third parties acting on our behalf, may collect information in the form of log files that detail website activities and gather statistics about user browsing habits. These logs are typically generated anonymously and help us understand:

  • The type of browser and operating system used by visitors;
  • Session details, including referring URL, date, time, pages visited, and duration of visits across our websites and compatible services.

Web Beacons: We may use web beacons (also known as transparent GIFs) on Clave websites. Web beacons are small pieces of code that allow a graphic image to be delivered on a web page for the purpose of transferring data back to us. We use web beacon information for various purposes, including:

  • Understanding how users respond to email campaigns;
  • Monitoring traffic across our websites and compatible services;
  • Counting unique visitors, auditing, reporting on advertising and email campaigns, and personalizing our websites and services

It is important to note that you are responsible for ensuring that your computer or mobile device settings reflect your preferences regarding the acceptance of cookies and similar technologies.

Most web browsers allow you to configure settings to notify you before accepting cookies or to refuse them altogether. While you can browse most Clave websites without cookies enabled, some features may not function properly. We recommend consulting your browser’s “Help” section for instructions on how to manage these settings. If you use different devices or browsers, you will need to configure your preferences on each one individually.

Because web beacons may be embedded within web pages, it is not possible to opt out of them directly. However, you can disable them by managing your cookie preferences, which may limit their functionality.
